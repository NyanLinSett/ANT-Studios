Additional Tips, and Resources

To study Vocabulary, and Terms, use Quizlet , Anki - Flashcards - which will aid you in writing and reading comprehension.

Use note taking apps like Notion and Roam Research to start a daily writing habit. This will not only improve your note-taking skills, but also help you cultivate a daily practice of crafting proses, as well as leave you with knowledge dots that you can connect, and expand further down your intellectual career.
Notion is also a great tool for managing your life, digitally- planning, tracking, organizing, collaborating, you have it all!

Last, but not least, make sure you're happy, and healthy. Physiology needs are the most fundamental needs, as Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs state, and the ultimate motivaton comes from inspiration and fascination, therefore, you can't improve your skiills, socialize well and be producive if you're not feeling well, and not your best!
