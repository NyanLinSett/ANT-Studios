Writing Tips

#1 Cultivate a flexible Writing style

Find out the type of style you like. After finding out your style, alter it in a way so that you'll be able to implement the writing style in whatever writing tasks and whatever forms- informal or formal- you face.

#2 Make Writing become a habit.

Whenever you get a chance or whenever you have something cool pop up in your imagination, try to see how you can turn that into a short story. It can just be a paragraph long. You improve your skills by doing it consistently and writing is no exception. Write, write, write and your writing style will improve by itself overtime.

#3 Read Widely, again.

Read varieties of written works, that way you'll be able to find the style of an author you enjoy and actually implement it in your own writing.

#4 practice typing fast. (Optional, for Online Education)

Many of us learn and take classes online nowadays so when you do have to write, instead of with a pen, you will be writing with a keyboard. That means that you need to practice your typing. You will not have enough time and you cannot work to your potential if you are struggling too much with typing your words out. That is why it is recommended for you to improve your typing speed.
