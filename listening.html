Listening Tips

#1 Consume entertainment that revolves around the language you want to improve.

The more you listen, the more you get familiar with the language and the words, and the more efficiently you'll be able to understand what someone is speaking.

#2 In listening tests, pay attention to the question.

Give attention to the sentences and phrases on the paper, thereby you'll be able to listen more carefully at what will actually be asked when the audio plays.

#3 Listen to and watch lectures and presentations.

Close your eyes, do not open subtitles and get familiar with the language and the words. Progress is slow at first, but at a certain point, you will progress much faster and become very used to the language.
